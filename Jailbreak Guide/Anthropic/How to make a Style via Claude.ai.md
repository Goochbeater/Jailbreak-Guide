**Prepare Your Instructions First**: Have your complete custom prompt written and tested before starting this process. The text field doesn't save drafts.

**Be Specific**: The more detailed your instructions, the better Claude will understand what you want. Include tone, formatting preferences, personality traits, and any special rules.

**Test Thoroughly**: After creation, send a few test messages to see if Claude responds according to your specifications. You can always edit the style later if needed.

**Use Multiple Styles**: You can create several different styles for different purposes‚Äîone for creative writing, another for professional work, etc.
